Al-Taweel Constructions, 18 Years Of Excellence

Welcome to Al-Taweel Constructions, your trusted partner in the construction industry. Based in the heart of Egypt and with a legacy dating back to 2005, we have been at the forefront of the construction sector, consistently delivering excellence in every project we undertake. Throughout our journey, we have forged enduring relationships with our clients, partners, and communities, solidifying our reputation as a reliable and forward-thinking construction company. As we continue to operate and grow within Egypt, we look forward to the opportunity to collaborate with you on your next construction endeavor.

Our Vison

Our vision is to reshape the skyline of Egypt and beyond with innovative construction solutions. We aspire to be the industry leader, renowned for our commitment to excellence, sustainability, and enriching the communities we serve.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver superior construction services, exceeding client expectations, and fostering long-term partnerships. We are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and integrity in every project. By investing in our people, technology, and sustainable practices, we aim to create enduring value for our clients and society.

Meet our Chairman

Yasser Ahmed A-Taweel, our esteemed Chairman, is a visionary leader who founded our company in 2005, initially in Qatar before bringing its legacy to Egypt. His unwavering dedication and tireless work ethic have been the driving forces behind our success. Born in the vibrant city of Cairo, Egypt, Yasser is not only a passionate entrepreneur but also a loving father of five. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Civil and Construction Engineering, earned from the University of Benha in Egypt, underscoring his strong foundation in the field and commitment to excellence in both his personal and professional life.
